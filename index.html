Who We Are

A New Wave of
Konkani Sound

Odessis is a next-gen music label evolving into a full-fledged movie studio — creating original Konkani sounds, cinematic narratives, and culture-driven entertainment for a global audience.

Born from the soul of Goa, Odessis bridges tradition with innovation. We believe the Konkani language and its stories deserve a world stage — and we're building that platform through music, film, and creative storytelling.

What We Do

Our Vision

From original music to cinematic storytelling, we're building a creative ecosystem rooted in Konkani culture.

Music Label

Producing and releasing original Konkani music across all major streaming platforms. From soulful ballads to high-energy dance tracks.

Film & Cinema

Evolving into a movie studio creating cinematic narratives that bring Konkani stories to life on the big screen and streaming platforms.

Artist Development

Nurturing Konkani artists, helping them find their voice and reach audiences beyond Goa's shores through production, branding, and distribution.

Cultural Storytelling

Preserving and modernizing Konkani heritage through music, visuals, and narrative-driven content that resonates across generations.

Music Production

Full-cycle production from concept to final master. State-of-the-art recording, mixing, mastering, and sound design services.

Global Distribution

Bringing Konkani music to the world through YouTube, Spotify, Apple Music, and every major digital platform.
